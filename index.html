The Right Paper for the Right Project

Materials

Tissue Foil

A double layer paper made with tissue and aluminum foil. The tissue profides malleability while the foil is durable. Very thin and useful for supercomplex models with many layers.

Kraft Paper

Cheap, takes creases easily, and available in large sizes. The most durable thin paper. Useful for experimentation and trial attempts.

Origamido

Very thin and durable, specifically hand made by Michael Lafosse and Richard Alexander for origami and chemical treatment.
